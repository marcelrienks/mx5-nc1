02 — Measurement Methodology

How Camber Is Measured

Instrument: Plumb line (weighted string) + string box reference frame
Measurement: Distance from plumb line to rim edge, taken at top and bottom of wheel
Calculation: (Top rim distance − Bottom rim distance) = camber offset in mm · Negative values = desired negative camber
Data gathered: 4 front positions (−6, 0, +6, +12 reversal) × 3 rear positions (−6, 0, +6) = 12 total measurements
