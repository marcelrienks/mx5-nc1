MX-5 NC1 — Recommended Eccentric Bolt Positions

🔗 FRONT CONSOLIDATED COMPROMISE: These recommended bolt positions are NOT isolated per wheel. They represent the best compromise for the front axle as a whole, prioritizing (1) symmetric string line measurement between left and right wheels, and (2) achieving target camber/caster values. Individual consolidated reports show per-wheel isolated optimums; those recommendations will differ from what's shown here due to symmetry enforcement across both wheels.
Front Left at −6 / Front Right at +6 (both at Rear −6) achieve −7mm symmetric measurement on both wheels. Left wheel sacrifices 2mm from its isolated best (−9mm achievable) to match right wheel's ceiling (−7mm). Individual wheel reports show isolated analysis; consolidated reports show per-corner trade-offs; this report shows front-wide symmetry priority.
